    ## Creating an Abstract Data Type to Organize Musical Data        
    <div class="forYouToDo">
        <ol start="4">
            <li>
                <div class="sidenoteBig">
                    <p><em>Pitch</em> is the amount of highness or lowness of a musical note; the pitch value goes in the first input slot of the <code>play note</code> block.</p>
                    <p>The <em>length</em> of a note is the amount of time that the note plays (the number of beats); the length value goes in the second slot of <code>play note</code>.</p>  
                </div>
